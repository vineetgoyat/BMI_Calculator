> A free, beautiful BMI calculator with detailed health analysis, pros & cons, and personalized improvement roadmaps.

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Accurate BMI Calculation** — metric & imperial units supported
- 📊 **Animated Gauge Meter** — visual BMI indicator with smooth animation
- ✅ **Pros & Cons Analysis** — what's working for you and what to watch out for
- 🚀 **Improvement Roadmap** — 6 personalized tips based on your BMI category
- 📅 **7-Day Weekly Plan** — suggested workout schedule tailored to your profile
- 🔥 **TDEE Calculator** — daily caloric needs based on activity level
- 💧 **Water Intake Guide** — recommended daily hydration
- 🎨 **Stunning Dark UI** — purple/pink glassmorphism with particle animations
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — semantic structure
- **Vanilla CSS** — glassmorphism, animations, gradients
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — zero dependencies, all logic from scratch
- **Canvas API** — particle animation system
