      Understanding BMI

      What the numbers really mean for your health

      📐

      What is BMI?

      Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from a person's weight and height. The formula is weight (kg) divided by height (m) squared.

      🎯

      Why It Matters

      BMI provides a reliable indicator of body fatness for most people and is used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

      ⚡

      Its Limitations

      BMI doesn't directly measure body fat. Athletes may have a high BMI but low fat. Always combine it with other health assessments.
